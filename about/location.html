Conference & Hachathon

droidcon Kraków 2015 is coming soon so we decided to put together some information for you about locations and tips on how to get there.

droidcon Kraków 2015 is going to be hosted by
Best Western PLUS Q Hotel
Wygrana 6 street in Kraków

Afterparty for droidcon Kraków 2015 will be at
Forum Przestrzenie
Marii Konopnickiej 28 street in Kraków

The Hackathon on Sunday will be hosted by
Base Lab in Tech Space
Wyczółkowskiego 7 street in Kraków

Travel from the Kraków Balice Airport

By TAXI

The easiest but most expensive way to reach the city center from the airport Kraków Balice is by a taxi, which can cost around 60-100 PLN.

By railway

You can also travel by train. It will take you around 20 minutes to reach Main Railway Station. Trains leave each 30 minutes from the stop Kraków Lotnisko/Airport

By bus

Or you can take a bus. Ticket to the city center costs 4.00 PLN and the trip will last around 45-50 minutes depending on traffic. Line numbers that you should look for are: 208, 292 or for the night line 902.

Moving around in the city

TAXI

You can use regular TAXIs which will cost you around 25 PLN per ride to move around the City Center.
It is also possible to use Uber in Kraków too.

Trams

Direct tram lines to the conference venue are 18, 52.
It will take around 20 minutes to get to the conference venue from Main Railway Station.
20 minutes ticket costs 2.80 PLN, but if you're not sure if you're gonna make it in this time, just take One Ride ticket for 3.80 PLN.
Leave the tram at the stop Centrum Konferencyjne (Conference Center).

Buses

Direct bus connections are eg. 164, 169, 503 or night lines 608, 610. Bus tickets are the same as tram tickets.
Leave the bus at the stop Centrum Konferencyjne (Conference Center).

See you at droidcon Kraków 2015! :)
